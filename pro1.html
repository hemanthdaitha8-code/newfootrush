❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place an order? ▼
To place an order, browse our collection, select your desired shoes, and click "Add to Cart". Once you've added all items, click the cart button to review and proceed to checkout. You can then enter your delivery details and payment information.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, mobile wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay, PayTM), and net banking options for your convenience.
What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 30-day return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the shoes within 30 days of delivery in their original condition with packaging. A full refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.
How long does shipping take? ▼
We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and express shipping (2-3 business days). The shipping time depends on your location and the shipping method selected during checkout.
Can I track my order? ▼
Yes! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your shipment in real-time on our website or the courier's website.
Do you offer size exchanges? ▼
Yes, we offer free size exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Simply contact our customer support team with your order number and the size you'd like to exchange for.
Is there a warranty on shoes? ▼
All shoes come with a 1-year manufacturing warranty covering defects in materials and craftsmanship. This warranty does not cover normal wear and tear or damage from misuse.
